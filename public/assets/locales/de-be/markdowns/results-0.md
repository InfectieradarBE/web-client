On March 30, 2021 we had 230 participants. 44.8% of the participants are female, and 55.2% male.
The majority of the participants (81.3%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.3% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 14.4% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.
